Ingredients (Cake):
- Flour, cocoa powder
- Baking soda, salt
- Sugar
- Eggs
- Buttermilk
- Vinegar
- Vanilla extract
- Red food coloring
- Butter

Ingredients (Cream Cheese Frosting):
- Cream cheese
- Butter
- Powdered sugar
- Vanilla extract

Instructions:
1. Mix dry cake ingredients.
2. Cream sugar and butter, add eggs.
3. Alternate adding dry ingredients and buttermilk to the batter.
4. Add vinegar, vanilla extract, and red food coloring.
5. Bake in layers, let cool.
6. Cream together cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract for frosting.
7. Frost the cooled cake.
