Ingredients:
- Tomatoes, diced
- Cucumbers, diced
- Kalamata olives
- Feta cheese, crumbled
- Red onion, thinly sliced
- Olive oil
- Oregano
- Lemon juice

Instructions:
1. In a bowl, combine tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, feta, and red onion.
2. Drizzle olive oil over the salad.
3. Sprinkle oregano and squeeze lemon juice.
4. Toss the salad gently.
5. Serve chilled.
