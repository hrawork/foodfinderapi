Ingredients:
- Spaghetti
- Pancetta or bacon
- Eggs
- Parmesan cheese
- Garlic
- Salt and black pepper

Instructions:
1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
2. In a pan, cook pancetta or bacon until crispy.
3. Whisk together eggs, Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, salt, and black pepper.
4. Drain cooked spaghetti and toss with egg mixture.
5. Add cooked pancetta or bacon.
6. Serve immediately.
