Ingredients:
- Cooked rice, cooled
- Vegetables (e.g., peas, carrots, corn)
- Protein (e.g., diced chicken, shrimp)
- Soy sauce
- Scrambled eggs
- Sesame oil

Instructions:
1. In a pan, saut√© vegetables and protein until cooked.
2. Add the cooled, cooked rice to the pan.
3. Pour soy sauce over the rice and mix well.
4. Push the rice to the side, add a little oil, and scramble an egg.
5. Mix the scrambled egg into the rice.
6. Drizzle with sesame oil and stir until well combined.
7. Serve hot.
