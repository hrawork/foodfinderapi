- Potatoes, boiled and mashed
- Peas
- Cumin seeds
- Coriander powder
- Garam masala
- Turmeric
- Chopped green chilies
- Ginger, grated
- Coriander leaves, chopped

Ingredients (Wrapper):
- Samosa wrappers
