Ingredients:
- Avocados
- Tomatoes, diced
- Red onion, finely chopped
- Cilantro, chopped
- Lime juice
- Salt and pepper
- Garlic, minced (optional)

Instructions:
1. Mash avocados in a bowl.
2. Add diced tomatoes, chopped red onion, and chopped cilantro.
3. Mix in lime juice, salt, pepper, and minced garlic (if using).
4. Adjust seasoning to taste.
5. Serve with tortilla chips.
