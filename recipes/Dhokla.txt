Ingredients:
- Chickpea flour (besan)
- Semolina (sooji)
- Yogurt
- Ginger-green chili paste
- Mustard seeds
- Curry leaves
- Baking soda
- Lemon juice
- Oil

Instructions:
1. Mix chickpea flour, semolina, yogurt, ginger-green chili paste, and salt to make a batter.
2. Add baking soda and lemon juice to the batter.
3. Steam the batter until it firms up.
4. Heat oil, add mustard seeds and curry leaves for tempering.
5. Pour the tempering over the steamed dhokla.
6. Cut into pieces and serve.
