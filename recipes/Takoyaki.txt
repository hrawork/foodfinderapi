Ingredients:
- Takoyaki batter mix (or make your own with flour, dashi, eggs, and salt)
- Cooked octopus pieces
- Green onions, chopped
- Tenkasu (tempura scraps)
- Bonito flakes
- Takoyaki sauce
- Japanese mayonnaise

Instructions:
1. Heat a takoyaki pan and oil each depression.
2. Pour batter into the depressions.
3. Add a piece of cooked octopus, green onions, and tenkasu to each.
4. Once the batter firms up, use skewers to turn the takoyaki to cook all sides.
5. Serve with takoyaki sauce, mayonnaise, and bonito flakes.
