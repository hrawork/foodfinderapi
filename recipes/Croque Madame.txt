Instructions:
1. Make a sandwich with ham and cheese between bread slices.
2. Butter the outside of the sandwich.
3. Grill or pan-fry until golden on both sides.
4. In a separate pan, fry an egg to your liking.
5. Place the fried egg on top of the sandwich.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
