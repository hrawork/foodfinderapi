Ingredients:
- Calamari rings
- Flour
- Salt and pepper
- Oil for frying
- Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:
1. Clean and slice the calamari into rings.
2. In a bowl, mix flour, salt, and pepper.
3. Coat the calamari rings with the flour mixture.
4. Heat oil in a pan or deep fryer to 350°F (175°C).
5. Fry the calamari until golden brown and crispy.
6. Remove from the oil, drain excess oil on paper towels.
7. Serve with lemon wedges.
