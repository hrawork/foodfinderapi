Ingredients:
- Potatoes
- Olive oil
- Salt
- Toppings of your choice (e.g., sour cream, cheese, chives)

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Wash and scrub potatoes.
3. Pierce potatoes with a fork, rub with olive oil, and sprinkle with salt.
4. Bake for about 1 hour until potatoes are tender.
5. Cut a slit in each potato and fluff the insides with a fork.
6. Add your favorite toppings.
