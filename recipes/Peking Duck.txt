Ingredients:
- Whole duck
- Soy sauce
- Hoisin sauce
- Chinese five-spice powder
- Ginger
- Garlic
- Pancakes
- Green onions, thinly sliced
- Cucumber, julienned

Instructions:
1. Clean and pat dry the duck.
2. Mix soy sauce, hoisin sauce, Chinese five-spice powder, grated ginger, and minced garlic to create a marinade.
3. Rub the marinade over the duck, including inside the cavity. Let it marinate for a few hours or overnight.
4. Roast the duck in the oven until the skin is crispy and the meat is cooked.
5. Serve the duck with thin pancakes, sliced green onions, and julienned cucumber.
