Ingredients (Dough):
- Flour
- Yeast
- Sugar
- Warm water
- Olive oil
- Salt

Ingredients (Toppings):
- Tomato sauce
- Mozzarella cheese
- Toppings of your choice (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers)

Instructions:
1. Dissolve sugar in warm water, add yeast and let it proof.
2. Mix flour and salt, add yeast mixture and olive oil.
3. Knead the dough until elastic, let it rise.
4. Roll out the dough, spread tomato sauce, add cheese and toppings.
5. Bake in a preheated oven until crust is golden.
