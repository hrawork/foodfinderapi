Ingredients:
- Corn tortillas
- Cooked and shredded chicken
- Shredded cheese
- Salsa
- Sour cream

Instructions:
1. Warm tortillas in a dry skillet.


2. Fill each tortilla with shredded chicken and cheese.
3. Roll tightly and secure with toothpicks.
4. Bake or fry until crispy.
5. Serve with salsa and sour cream.
