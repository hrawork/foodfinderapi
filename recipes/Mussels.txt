Ingredients:
- Fresh mussels
- Garlic, minced
- Onion, chopped
- Tomatoes, diced
- White wine
- Parsley, chopped
- Butter

Instructions:
1. Clean and debeard the mussels.
2. In a pot, saut√© garlic and onions in butter.
3. Add diced tomatoes and cook until softened.
4. Pour in white wine and bring to a simmer.
5. Add the mussels, cover, and cook until they open.
6. Discard any unopened mussels.
7. Sprinkle chopped parsley and serve.
