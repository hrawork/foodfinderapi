Ingredients (Dough):
- Flour
- Sugar
- Yeast
- Milk
- Butter
- Egg
- Vanilla extract

Ingredients (Glaze):
- Powdered sugar
- Milk
- Vanilla extract

Instructions:
1. Mix dry dough ingredients.
2. Heat milk and melt butter, then add to dry ingredients along with beaten egg and vanilla.
3. Knead dough until smooth, let it rise.
4. Roll out dough and cut into donut shapes.
5. Fry until golden brown.
6. Mix glaze ingredients and dip donuts into the glaze.
