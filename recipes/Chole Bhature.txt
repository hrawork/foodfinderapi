Ingredients (Chole):
- Chickpeas
- Onion, tomatoes, ginger, garlic (chopped)
- Chole masala
- Turmeric, red chili powder, cumin powder
- Garam masala
- Amchur (dried mango powder)

Ingredients (Bhature):
- Flour
- Semolina
- Yogurt
- Baking powder
- Salt

Instructions:
1. Cook chickpeas and set aside.
2. Saut√© onions, tomatoes, ginger, and garlic.
3. Add spices and cooked chickpeas, cook until flavors meld.
4. For bhature, mix flour, semolina, yogurt, baking powder, and salt.
5. Make a soft dough, let it rest.
6. Roll out and deep fry until puffed.
