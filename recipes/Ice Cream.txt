Ingredients:
- Heavy cream
- Whole milk
- Sugar
- Vanilla extract

Instructions:
1. In a saucepan, heat heavy cream, whole milk, and sugar.
2. Stir until the sugar dissolves.
3. Remove from heat and add vanilla extract.
4. Let the mixture cool, then refrigerate.
5. Once cold, churn in an ice cream maker according to the manufacturer's instructions.
6. Transfer to a container and freeze until firm.
7. Scoop and serve.
