Ingredients:
- Potatoes
- Vegetable oil
- Salt

 Instructions:
1. Peel and cut potatoes into thin strips.
2. Rinse the cut potatoes in cold water to remove excess starch.
3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or a large pan to 350°F (175°C).
4. Carefully add the potatoes to the hot oil and fry until golden brown.
5. Remove fries from oil, place on paper towels to drain excess oil.
6. Season with salt and serve hot.
