Ingredients (Cupcakes):
- Flour
- Baking powder
- Sugar
- Eggs
- Milk
- Vanilla extract
- Butter

Ingredients (Buttercream Frosting):
- Butter
- Powdered sugar
- Vanilla extract
- Milk

Instructions:
1. Mix dry cupcake ingredients.
2. Cream sugar and butter, add eggs.
3. Alternate adding dry ingredients and milk to the batter.
4. Add vanilla extract.
5. Bake in cupcake liners, let cool.
6. Cream together butter, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and milk for frosting.
7. Frost the cooled cupcakes.
