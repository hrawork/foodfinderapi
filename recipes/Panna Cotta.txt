Ingredients:
- Heavy cream
- Sugar
- Vanilla extract
- Gelatin
- Water

Instructions:
1. In a saucepan, heat heavy cream and sugar until sugar dissolves.
2. Remove from heat, add vanilla extract.
3. In a separate bowl, sprinkle gelatin over water and let it bloom.
4. Add the bloomed gelatin to the cream mixture, stir until dissolved.
5. Pour the mixture into molds or ramekins.
6. Refrigerate until set, usually for a few hours.
7. Unmold onto plates and serve chilled.
