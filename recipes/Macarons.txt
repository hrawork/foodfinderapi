Ingredients (Shells):
- Almond flour
- Powdered sugar
- Egg whites
- Granulated sugar
- Food coloring (optional)

Ingredients (Filling):
- Butter
- Powdered sugar
- Flavoring (e.g., vanilla extract, fruit puree)

Instructions:
1. Sift almond flour and powdered sugar together.
2. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites until foamy, gradually add granulated sugar until stiff peaks form.
3. Gently fold dry ingredients into the egg whites.
4. Add food coloring if desired.
5. Pipe small rounds onto a baking sheet and let them rest.
6. Bake until shells have formed and are not sticky.
7. Cream together butter, powdered sugar, and flavoring for the filling.
8. Sandwich pairs of shells with the filling.
