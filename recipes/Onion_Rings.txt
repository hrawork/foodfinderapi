Ingredients:
- Onions, sliced into rings
- Flour
- Salt and pepper
- Milk
- Oil for frying

Instructions:
1. Separate onion rings.
2. In a bowl, mix flour, salt, and pepper.
3. Dip onion rings in milk, then coat with the flour mixture.
4. Heat oil in a pan or deep fryer to 375°F (190°C).
5. Fry onion rings until golden brown.
6. Remove from oil, drain excess oil on paper towels.
7. Serve hot.
