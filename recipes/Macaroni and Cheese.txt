Ingredients:
- Elbow macaroni
- Butter
- Flour
- Milk
- Cheddar cheese, grated
- Salt and pepper
- Bread crumbs (optional)

Instructions:
1. Cook macaroni according to package instructions.
2. In a saucepan, make a roux with butter and flour.
3. Gradually whisk in milk until smooth.
4. Add grated cheddar cheese and stir until melted.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Mix cheese sauce with cooked macaroni.
7. Optionally, top with bread crumbs and bake until golden.
