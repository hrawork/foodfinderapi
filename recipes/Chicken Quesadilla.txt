Ingredients:
- Flour tortillas
- Cooked chicken, shredded
- Shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack)
- Salsa
- Sour cream
- Green onions, chopped (optional)

Instructions:
1. Place a tortilla in a pan over medium heat.
2. Spread shredded chicken and cheese on half of the tortilla.
3. Fold the other half over the filling.
4. Cook until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is golden.
5. Repeat with additional quesadillas.
6. Serve with salsa, sour cream, and chopped green onions.
