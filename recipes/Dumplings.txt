Ingredients (Filling):
- Ground meat (pork, chicken, or beef)
- Cabbage, finely chopped
- Garlic, minced
- Ginger, grated
- Soy sauce
- Sesame oil
- Green onions, chopped

Ingredients (Wrapper):
- Dumpling wrappers

Instructions:
1. Mix all filling ingredients together.
2. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of a dumpling wrapper.
3. Moisten the edge of the wrapper and fold in half, sealing the edges.
4. Steam or pan-fry until cooked.
