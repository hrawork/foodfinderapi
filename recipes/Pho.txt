Ingredients:
- Beef bones
- Onion, halved
- Ginger, sliced
- Star anise, cloves, cinnamon stick
- Rice noodles
- Beef slices
- Bean sprouts, basil, lime, chili for garnish

Instructions:
1. Roast beef bones in the oven.
2. In a large pot, combine bones, onion, ginger, star anise, cloves, and cinnamon. Simmer for several hours to make the broth.
3. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions.
4. Thinly slice beef.
5. Pour hot broth over noodles and beef.
6. Serve with bean sprouts, basil, lime, and chili.
